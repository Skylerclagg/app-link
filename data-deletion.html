Data Deletion

How to request deletion of your data from RoboNexus

What data does RoboNexus store?

RoboNexus only stores data on our servers if you enable push notifications:

  • Device push token — a unique identifier assigned by Apple or Google to deliver notifications to your device
  • Favorite teams — the teams you have selected to receive notifications for
  • Notification preferences — your notification settings within the app

This data is not linked to your identity. No account, name, email, or personal information is collected.

Delete within the app

  1. Open RoboNexus on your device
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Disable Push Notifications by turning off the notification toggle
  4. Your device token, favorite teams, and notification preferences are immediately deleted from our servers

What happens when data is deleted?

  • Deleted immediately: Device push token, favorite team selections, and notification preferences are permanently removed from our servers
  • Never stored on our servers: Location data, app settings, score calculator data, and all other app data remain only on your device
